Open Submissions

Write for the
Biology Club Magazine

Have a research idea, a topic you're passionate about, or a discovery you want to share? The magazine is open to all Biology Club members. We accept original articles, systematic reviews, opinion pieces, and lab reports. Your work will be peer-reviewed by fellow members and published for the entire community.

Submission Guidelines

500–2,000 words per article
Must be original, unpublished work
Cite all sources (APA or Vancouver)
Submit as a Google Doc or .docx
Peer review takes 5–7 business days
Open to all Biology Club members
